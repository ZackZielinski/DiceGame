Arena Battle

Roll the 'Attack' die to see how much damage you will perfrom. Roll the 'Defend' die to see how much your enemy will be blocking for.

Use the Calculate button to not only to have the math be done for you, but also to see if there was a counter attack (counterattacks only apply to player versus enemies, not other players). Once you have defeated your current target, proceed to the rewards page to claim your points.

Now it's your opponent's turn to strike. Roll the 'Enemy attack' die to see how much they hit you for. Roll the 'Damage Blocked' die to know how much damage you are protecting. (Counterattacks are not calculated in player versus player matches)
