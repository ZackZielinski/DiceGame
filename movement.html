Sarting the Game

Roll the die in order to determine how many battle spaces you will be required to make depending upon how the board size will be.

This game utilizes a directional die to determine which way you will be moving (North, South, East, West).

If you ever land on a Battle Space, you must roll to see what enemy you will be facing. Your turn ends on the first attack and you will not move until the creature is slain.
